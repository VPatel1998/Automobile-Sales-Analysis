## üóÇÔ∏è Dataset Description
The dataset contains detailed historical data on automobile sales and several economic and market variables, both in recession and non-recession periods:
- **Date**: Date of observation  
- **Recession**: Binary flag (1=recession, 0=normal)  
- **Automobile_Sales**: Number of vehicles sold  
- **GDP**: Per capita GDP (USD)  
- **Unemployment_Rate**: Monthly unemployment rate  
- **Consumer_Confidence**: Synthetic consumer confidence index  
- **Seasonality_Weight**: Weight representing seasonal effects on sales  
- **Price**: Average vehicle price  
- **Advertising_Expenditure**: Company advertising expenses  
- **Vehicle_Type**: Categories including Supermini, Small Family Car, Medium Family Car, Executive Car, Sports  
- **Competition**: Market competition measure (competitors/market share)  
- **Month**: Extracted month from Date  
- **Year**: Extracted year from Date
